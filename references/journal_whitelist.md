# Journal Whitelist Notes

Journal whitelist configuration lives in:

- `config/journals_zh.yml`
- `config/journals_en.yml`

The whitelist is intentionally editable. It is a source-quality signal, not a complete ranking system.

For Chinese journals, whether a journal is listed in Peking University Core Journals, CSSCI, AMI, or other sources must be manually verified and maintained by the user. The scripts do not invent or infer core-source status.

For English journals, fields such as JCR quartile, CiteScore, SJR, ABS rating, or impact factor should be added only after manual verification from licensed or official sources. The scripts do not fabricate impact metrics.
